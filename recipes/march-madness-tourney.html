MARCH MADNESS TOURNEY

Description

The NCAA has a basketball tournament that takes place at the end of each conferences individual tournaments. This tournament is commonly referred to as "March Madness" and is recognized as the greatest sporting event of the modern age. This recipe will outline all of the necessary ingredients that ensure the tournament produces mind-shattering excitement.

Ingredients

  • Unpredictable MAJOR upsets
  • One incredible cinderalla story or 2-3 minor low seed runs
  • Buzzer beaters, one posession games, and more buzzer beaters
  • Programs making history for their school or for the NCAA tournament
  • Epic semi finals and championship game

Steps

This section will define the ingredients mentioned in the previous section.

  1. Every year, analysts and amateurs boldly claim they know which teams are "dangerous", and every year, people are shocked and brackets are busted within the first day of games because an underdog won a game that very few people forsaw.
  2. One or more teams will inevitably go against all odds and upset multiple teams to progess much farther in the tournament than anyone would have guessed. This is knows as a cinderellas story - when the unlikely winning streak is made my massive underdogs - or simply as a run when does by minor underdogs.
  3. Many of the games will come down to the wire to be decided with the final shot of the game. Usually the shots miss, but they seem to hit at a higher rate during the month of march.
  4. Since this tournament is relatively young, there are still many firsts to be had. Schools making the tournament for the first time, winning a game in the tournament for the first time, or securing the champtionship for the first time are some of the very very few examples of history that can be made and will be made every year in this event.
  5. Finally, the last three games of the tournament will be good games.
